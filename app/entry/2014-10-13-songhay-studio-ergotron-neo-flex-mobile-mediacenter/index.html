Songhay Studio: Ergotron Neo-Flex Mobile MediaCenter

A YouTube video from Geek Beat says it all to me: Ergotron products continue to rock! The Neo-Flex Mobile MediaCenter is a welcome addition to my sense of studio space. I used to look down on looking at a big ass monitor but now at my age—with my current quality of eyesight—I realize I was wrong for being so haughty.
